Perception Album

About Perception

Perception is the third studio album released by the artist NF, released on October 6th, 2017. The album reached the number one spot on the Billboard 200. There are sixteen songs on the album, including his most popular song "Let You Down" which has been triple platinum certified.

Facts and Statistics

  • Outro was released on August 2nd, 2017 as the first single on the upcoming album
  • Let You Down was released three days before the album and would be his first song to chart on the Billboard Hot 100
  • Perception debuted at Number One on the US Billboard 200
  • Let You Down is certified triple platinum
